- IG Reels: 9:16 vertical video, 15–30 sec, text overlay + voiceover

## TIKTOK ADS (6 Variants)

### Format guidance
- 9:16 vertical video, 15–60 sec
- Hook must land in first 1–2 seconds
- Text overlays + trending audio (instrumental only — avoid copyrighted songs)
- Send to `/founding` with UTM: `?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=founding&utm_content=[variant]`
- TikTok policy: do NOT reference "adult," "hookup," "sexual," or similar. Stick to luxury/exclusivity angle.

---

### TT-01 — "POV: You got the invite"
**Hook (0–2 sec):** "POV: You just got invited to something you're not allowed to talk about."  
**Primary Text (on-screen overlay, builds line by line):**  
"An exclusive members club."  
"Real events. Real connections."  
"No apps. No algorithms."  
"Only 100 founding spots."  
"CA$39 — before the price triples."  
"Link in bio."  
**CTA:** Link in bio → `/founding`  
**Audio:** Lo-fi cinematic instrumental, dark and moody  
**Visual Concept:** Slow-panning shots: black gloves picking up a sealed envelope, amber wax seal, elegant interior space. Creator-style UGC aesthetic (handheld, slightly shaky) to blend with organic TikTok feed. Very dark color grade.

---

### TT-02 — "What is Paixão?"

"It's an invite-only members club for adults who want curated experiences."

"After that, the price goes to CA$139. Then CA$499. Then it closes."

**CTA:** Link in bio  
**Visual Concept:** Either a creator speaking directly to camera in a dark, elegant setting (low-key lighting, neutral background) or text-card style with amber/black color palette and Playfair Display typography. Minimal movement — luxury aesthetic resists frantic cuts.
