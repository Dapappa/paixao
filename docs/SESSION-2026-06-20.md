## Locked brand system (the rules — do NOT regress)
- **Palette 70/25/5:** near-black `#0a0a0a` field · warm gold `#d4a574` for headings/detail ·
  crimson `#c2185b` as a **rationed single drop** (primary CTA fill, active state only).
- **Headline accents are GOLD** (`text-gradient-brand` was redefined to gold in globals.css).
  Crimson is NOT used for big text/areas.
- **No AI-slop:** killed hot-pink `#e91e63` gradient → deep velvet; accent-hover → `#d81b60`
  (more light, not a new hue); de-rainbowed status + chart tokens. No multi-hue gradients,
  no confetti (removed from hero-mockup → gold ring-pulse), no emoji.
- **Type:** Playfair Display headings at **font-medium, never bold** (bold kills the Didone
  contrast); responsive `clamp()` so nothing overflows mobile. Body = Hanken Grotesk.
- **Motion:** breath/heartbeat, ease `[0.05,0.7,0.1,1]`, never bouncy. All decorative motion
  gated on `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- **No dashes:** user dislikes `·` middots AND `—` em-dashes. Removed in Why Paixão,
  How It Works, hero disclaimers, founding. **Still present on:** home hero body, about,
  founding body copy — a site-wide em-dash sweep was OFFERED but not yet done.
